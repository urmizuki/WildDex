THE BIO-IDENTIFIER
THE HOOK

Malaysia has some of the world's most biodiverse forests.

15,000+

species of flowering plants and trees

But we don't have enough experts to identify them.

THE PROBLEM

EXPERT SHORTAGE

There are only a handful of dendrology specialists in Malaysia. Field surveys that should take weeks stretch into months waiting for expert identification.

SLOWED RESEARCH

Biodiversity auditing, carbon stock assessments, and conservation planning all depend on reliable species identification — the bottleneck is human expertise.

WEAK ENFORCEMENT

Without on-site experts, illegal logging of protected species often goes undetected. PERHILITAN and FRIM need tools their field staff can use.

THE SOLUTION

WILDDEX is an AI-powered "Point-and-Identify" mobile app that turns tree species identification into a gamified collection experience.

Point your phone at a tree. Get instant species ID. Collect trading cards. Build Malaysia's largest crowd-sourced forest database.
